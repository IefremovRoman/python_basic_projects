Welcome to BlackJack game!

Rules are simple: make more scores than opponent, but not exceeded 21. You`ve got 21 scores and you are automatically winner!

Gameplay:
1. Your name
2. Number of decks (hearts, diamonds, spades, clubs x Number of decks)
3. Make a bet
4. Play (2 cards start, then choose either you need next one etc, Hit, or Stand)
5. You win, if you get BlackJat (Face and Ace) - get your bet x 1.5 back!
   You win, if you get more than Croupier - get your bet x 1 back!
   You loose, if you get less than Croupier! Then go away and play again!
6. Play until you have money

Enjoy!
